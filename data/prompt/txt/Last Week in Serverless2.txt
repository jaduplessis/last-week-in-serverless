Here's a brief run-down on the announcements from the last week that piqued my interest.

Announcing Terraform support for Amazon OpenSearch Ingestion deployments("https://aws.amazon.com/about-aws/whats-new/2024/02/amazon-opensearch-ingestion-deployments-terraform/")
Starting on 02/28/2024, you can automate and secure the deployment of Amazon OpenSearch Ingestion pipelines across 15 regions using Terraform, enhancing efficiency and reducing errors.

Experience up to 40% faster stack creation with CloudFormation("https://aws.amazon.com/about-aws/whats-new/2024/03/aws-cloudformation-40-percent-faster-stack-creation/")
(Stolen from Josh) AWS has increased stack creation using CloudFormation by 40% and introduced a new stack creation event, CONFIGURATION_COMPLETE.

Building For Interactivity: When Async Is Not Enough("https://www.readysetcloud.io/blog/allen.helton/async-is-not-enough/")
Allen Helton recently released a new article that discusses the importance of real-time notifications in modern software development, advocating for the use of asynchronous communication to improve user experiences by eliminating wait times.

Anthropic’s Claude 3 Sonnet foundation model is now available in Amazon Bedrock("https://aws.amazon.com/blogs/aws/anthropics-claude-3-sonnet-foundation-model-is-now-available-in-amazon-bedrock/")
The article discusses the strategic collaboration between AWS and Anthropic to accelerate the development and accessibility of Anthropic's Claude foundation models on Amazon Bedrock

Vector engine for Amazon OpenSearch Serverless is now available("https://aws.amazon.com/blogs/aws/vector-engine-for-amazon-opensearch-serverless-is-now-generally-available/")
There is general availability of the vector engine for Amazon OpenSearch Serverless("https://aws.amazon.com/opensearch-service/serverless-vector-engine/") with new features!
